# Navigating DocJacket

Here's a quick tour of the DocJacket layout so you know where everything lives.

## The Dashboard

The dashboard is your home base. When you log in, you'll see:

- **Today's Briefing** — A snapshot of your day: closings today, tasks due, overdue items, and your overall workload level
- **Quick Actions** — Shortcuts to common tasks like creating a transaction or uploading a contract
- **Closing Soon** — Transactions approaching their closing date, with urgency indicators
- **Quick Tasks** — Your most pressing to-dos across all transactions
- **Month Stats** — New transactions, closed deals, active volume, and pending tasks for the month

## The Sidebar

The sidebar on the left is your main navigation. Here's what each section does:

| Menu Item | What It Does |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Dashboard** | Your daily overview and quick actions |
| **Transactions** | All your deals — search, filter, and manage |
| **Tasks** | Organization-wide task list across all transactions |
| **Pipeline** | Visual pipeline view of your deals by status |
| **Templates** | Create and manage timeline, task, and email templates |
| **Inbox** | Your message center for transaction-related emails |
| **Contacts** | Your contact database — clients, agents, vendors |
| **Calendar** | See all milestones and deadlines in a calendar view |
| **Listings** | Property listings you're tracking |
| **Offices** | Manage brokerage offices |
| **Reports** | Transaction reports and analytics |
| **Billing** | Your subscription and payment details |
| **Settings** | Organization settings, user management, integrations |

## Inside a transaction

When you open a transaction, you'll see a tabbed layout:

- **Overview** — Everything at a glance: key dates, financials, contacts, tasks, documents, milestones, contingencies, and repairs
- **Timeline** — Your milestones and deadlines with status tracking
- **Tasks** — To-do list specific to this transaction
- **Documents** — All files for this deal
- **Contacts** — People involved in this transaction
- **Messages** — Email threads related to this deal
- **Notes** — Internal notes for your team (not visible to clients)

The **Overview** tab is where most TCs spend their time — it's a single-page view of everything important about the deal.

## Quick tips

- **Search** is everywhere — use the search bar on the Transactions page to find deals by address, buyer name, or seller name
- **Help chat** — Click the chat button in the bottom-right corner anytime for instant AI-powered help
- **Keyboard shortcuts** — Press `?` to see available keyboard shortcuts on any page
- **Dark mode** — Toggle dark mode in Settings if you prefer working at night
