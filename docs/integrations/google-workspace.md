# Google Workspace

Connect your Google account to sync email, documents, and calendar events with DocJacket.

## What's included

### Gmail
- Send emails from DocJacket using your Gmail account
- Incoming emails are tracked and linked to transactions
- Email history syncs both ways

### Google Drive
- Import documents from Drive into any transaction
- Export documents from DocJacket back to Drive
- Browse your Drive folders directly from the Documents tab

### Google Calendar
- Sync transaction milestones and deadlines to your Google Calendar
- See DocJacket events alongside your personal calendar
- Two-way sync — changes in either place are reflected in both

## Connecting

1. Go to **Settings > Integrations**
2. Find **Google Workspace** and click **Connect**
3. Sign in with your Google account
4. Grant the requested permissions
5. You're connected

## Tips

- Use a dedicated Google account for your TC business if you want to keep personal and work email separate
- Google Drive imports are copies — changes in Drive won't sync to DocJacket automatically
- Calendar sync is especially useful if you manage your schedule through Google Calendar
