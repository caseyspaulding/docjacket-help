# Microsoft Outlook

Connect your Microsoft 365 account to sync email, calendar, and documents with DocJacket.

## What's included

### Outlook Mail
- Send emails from DocJacket using your Outlook account
- Incoming emails are tracked and linked to transactions
- Email history syncs both ways

### Outlook Calendar
- Sync transaction milestones and deadlines to your Outlook Calendar
- See DocJacket events alongside your existing schedule

### OneDrive
- Store and sync transaction documents with OneDrive
- Access your cloud files directly from DocJacket

## Connecting

1. Go to **Settings > Integrations**
2. Find **Microsoft 365** and click **Connect**
3. Sign in with your Microsoft account
4. Grant the requested permissions
5. You're connected

## Tips

- Works with Microsoft 365 business and personal accounts
- Calendar sync is two-way — changes in either place are reflected in both
- If your organization uses Microsoft 365 for email, this integration keeps everything in sync without switching between apps
