# Client Portal

<VideoPlaceholder>See what your clients experience when they open their portal link</VideoPlaceholder>

The Client Portal gives your buyers, sellers, and agents a branded transaction overview — without requiring a DocJacket account. They can see key dates, shared documents, portal-visible tasks, transaction details, service providers, and status updates through a secure link you send them.

Portal links are recipient-specific. A transaction can have separate buyer/seller, agent, and vendor links, and agents can also receive an agent hub link that shows all of their active transactions in one place.

It's a professional touch that keeps everyone informed and cuts down on "where are we?" calls and emails.

![Client portal overview with branded header, transaction status, key dates, summary details, and parties](/img/client-portal/client-portal-overview.png)

The portal opens as a read-only transaction status page. The top of the page orients the viewer with your branding, the property address, status badge, print/download actions, and the most important transaction sections.

## Key features

- **Branded experience** — Customize the portal with your logo and colors
- **Secure access** — Each portal link is unique and can be set to expire
- **Track engagement** — See visits, unique visitors, document downloads, and recent access activity
- **Selective sharing** — Control which documents and tasks are visible on the portal
- **Multi-property support** — If the same contact has portal access to multiple active deals, they can switch between properties
- **Calendar and summary tools** — Viewers can copy a live calendar feed, print the portal, or download a branded one-page summary
