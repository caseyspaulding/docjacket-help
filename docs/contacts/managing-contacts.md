# Managing Contacts

## Adding a contact

From the **Contacts** page, click **Add Contact** and enter their details:

- Name, email, phone
- Company
- Contact type (agent, lender, client, etc.)
- Notes

You can also create contacts on the fly when adding someone to a transaction — just fill in their info and they're saved to your directory automatically.

## Searching and filtering

- **Search** by name, email, phone, or company
- **Filter by type** — Narrow down to agents, attorneys, lenders, clients, or any other contact type
- **Advanced filters** — Filter by transaction count, license status, and more
- **Customize columns** — Show or hide table columns to display exactly what you need

## Importing contacts

Have an existing contact list? Click **Add Contact > Import from CSV** to bulk-import contacts. Map your CSV columns to DocJacket fields and import.

## Exporting contacts

Click **Export** to download your contacts as a CSV file — useful for backup, reporting, or moving data between systems.
