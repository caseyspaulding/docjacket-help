# Contact Roles

When you add a contact to a transaction, you assign them a role that describes their part in the deal.

## Role categories

Contacts are organized into groups on the transaction overview:

**Primary (Clients)**
- Buyer, Seller

**Professional (Agents)**
- Listing Agent, Buying Agent

**Legal & Title**
- Title Company, Closing Attorney, Escrow Officer

**Lending**
- Lender, Loan Officer

**Service Providers**
- Home Inspector, Appraiser, Surveyor, Contractor, Insurance Agent

**Other**
- Property Manager, HOA Contact, and custom roles

## Why roles matter

Roles do more than just label contacts:

- **Portal visibility** — What a contact sees on the portal depends on their role (buyers see different info than agents)
- **Permissions** — Roles control what contacts can access
- **Communication** — Email templates can target contacts by role (e.g., "send to all agents")
- **Organization** — Roles help you see at a glance who's involved and in what capacity

## Custom roles

If the built-in roles don't cover your needs, you can create custom contact roles in **Settings > Transaction Roles**.
