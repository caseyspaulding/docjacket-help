# Plans & Pricing

DocJacket offers simple per-seat pricing. Every user on your team gets a seat, and you can add or remove seats as your team changes.

## What's included

All paid plans include the full DocJacket feature set:

- Unlimited transactions
- AI contract extraction and task generation
- Checklists and key date templates
- Document management and DocDrop
- Client portal
- Email integration (Gmail and Outlook)
- Calendar sync
- Reports and pipeline view
- Team management and roles

## Free plan

The free plan is a great way to try DocJacket before committing. It includes:

- **2 active transactions**, with AI contract extraction on them
- **Up to 5 team members**
- Unlimited intake forms and unlimited client & agent portals
- Email automation, templates, and checklist templates

Upgrade to a paid plan for unlimited active transactions and AI extractions, SMS messaging, a custom domain, MCP access, and priority support.

## Seats and team members

DocJacket uses **per-seat** pricing — but not everyone you work with needs a paid seat. This is the most common point of confusion, so here's how it works:

- **A seat is a team member who logs in** to run transactions — you, your coordinators, an assistant. On the **free plan you get up to 5 team members at no cost**; on a paid plan, each active team member is a seat.
- **You only pay for active seats.** Add or remove seats anytime from **Settings → Team Members**, and billing adjusts automatically.
- **Portal guests are always free.** The clients *and agents* you share a [portal link](../client-portal/index.mdx) with do **not** count as seats — no matter how many.

So *"is adding an agent free?"* depends on how you add them:

- **As a portal recipient** (you just want to give the agent visibility into their deals) → **always free**. Invite them to the [Client Portal](../client-portal/index.mdx).
- **As a logged-in team member** (they'll work inside DocJacket) → they use one of your free team-member slots on the free plan, or a paid seat on a paid plan.

See [Understanding Roles](../getting-started/understanding-roles.mdx) for what each team member can do.

## Early-bird pricing

If you signed up during the early-bird period, your locked-in rate stays the same for as long as you maintain your subscription. This rate is grandfathered and won't increase.
