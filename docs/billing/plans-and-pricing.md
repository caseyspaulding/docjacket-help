# Plans & Pricing

DocJacket offers simple per-seat pricing. Every user on your team gets a seat, and you can add or remove seats as your team changes.

## What's included

All paid plans include the full DocJacket feature set:

- Unlimited transactions
- AI contract extraction and task generation
- Timeline and task templates
- Document management and DocDrop
- Client portal
- Email integration (Gmail and Outlook)
- Calendar sync
- Reports and pipeline view
- Team management and roles

## Free plan

DocJacket offers a free plan with limited active transactions — great for trying out the platform before committing.

## Current pricing

Visit [docjacket.com/pricing](https://docjacket.com/pricing) for the latest pricing details and plan options.

## Early-bird pricing

If you signed up during the early-bird period, your locked-in rate stays the same for as long as you maintain your subscription. This rate is grandfathered and won't increase.
