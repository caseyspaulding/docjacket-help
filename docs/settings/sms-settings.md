# SMS Settings

Use **Settings > SMS** to manage texting for your organization.

## Business number

Organizations can use a dedicated business phone number for outbound SMS.

From SMS settings, you can:

- Search available phone numbers by area code
- Choose a number
- Provision the selected number
- See the city or region associated with the number when available

If you do not have a dedicated number, DocJacket can fall back to a shared DocJacket sending number.

## Sending and replies

To send a direct SMS from a transaction, open **Messages > Direct > New Message**.

Replies return to the same message thread automatically, so the conversation stays attached to the transaction.

Portal chat is separate from SMS and does not count against SMS usage.

## Usage and credits

SMS settings show monthly usage and the number of texts included in your plan.

If your included texts are used up, you can buy more credits from the SMS settings page. Credits are added to the current month.

## Releasing a number

The **Danger Zone** appears when your organization has a dedicated number.

Releasing a number permanently gives it up. Contacts who saved that number will no longer recognize your texts, and the same number may not be available again. Your account falls back to the shared DocJacket number after release.
