# Brand and Profile

Use **Settings > Brand** for organization-wide presentation and **Settings > Profile** for your personal account details.

## Brand settings

Brand settings control the look of DocJacket surfaces that clients, agents, and your team see.

From **Settings > Brand**, you can:

- Choose a brand preset or customize individual colors and fonts
- Save changes that flow across intake forms, Front Door, email templates, and the services manual
- Revert unsaved edits before saving
- Control whether "Powered by DocJacket" appears on public pages when your plan allows that setting

Brand changes preview in the admin experience as you edit, then apply to the organization once you click **Save Brand**.

## Profile settings

Use **Settings > Profile** to keep your own user details current.

You can update:

- Profile photo
- First and last name
- Phone number
- Real estate license number
- Timezone
- SMS reminder consent

Your email address is managed by your sign-in provider and cannot be changed from the profile page.

## Timezone

Your timezone affects how dates and reminders display for you. Use **Detect from browser** if you want DocJacket to choose the timezone from your current browser settings.

## Email forwarding

The profile page also shows your organization's intake email when email forwarding is active. Forwarding messages to that address lets DocJacket process inbound transaction documents for the organization.
