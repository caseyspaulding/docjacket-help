# Organization Settings

Customize DocJacket to match your brand and workflow preferences.

## Company information

Set your organization's basic info:

- **Company name** — Appears in emails, reports, and the client portal
- **Address and phone** — Contact details for your organization
- **Logo** — Uploaded logo appears on the portal, reports, and outgoing communications

## Branding

Your brand identity carries through DocJacket:

- **Logo** — Used in the client portal, email headers, and reports
- **Colors** — Customize accent colors for the portal

## Default settings

Configure defaults that apply to new transactions:

- **Default transaction type** — Pre-select the most common type
- **Default timeline template** — Auto-apply a template to new transactions
- **Custom fields** — Add custom fields to capture data specific to your workflow

## Transaction roles

In **Settings > Transaction Roles**, you can create and manage the contact roles available when adding contacts to a transaction. The built-in roles (Buyer, Seller, Agent, etc.) cover most needs, but you can add custom ones if your workflow requires it.
