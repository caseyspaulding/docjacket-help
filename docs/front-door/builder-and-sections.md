# Builder and Sections

The Front Door builder controls the public page visitors see. Use it to create the page layout, reorder sections, preview the page, publish or unpublish it, and track basic engagement.

## Starter templates

If your Front Door has no sections yet, click **Choose a starter template**. DocJacket can seed a starting layout for:

- TC
- Agent
- Broker
- Mix

Each starter shows the sections it will create before you continue. After the starter is created, you can edit, reorder, hide, or remove any section.

## Section blocks

Front Door sections are modular blocks. Current section types include:

- **Hero** — Logo, name, title, tagline, and first impression
- **Primary CTA** — The main button you want visitors to click
- **Secondary actions** — Up to three smaller CTA buttons
- **Services** — Your public offerings catalog
- **Packages** — Bundled service packages
- **Testimonials** — Client quotes
- **Share card** — QR code, copy link, and vCard contact save
- **About** — Bio and photo
- **Contact** — Phone, text, email, social links, address, and map
- **Brokerage** — Brokerage name and logo
- **Credentials** — Designations and certifications
- **Service area** — Cities, counties, or zip codes you cover
- **Social proof** — Public stats such as transactions closed or years of experience
- **Active listings** — Listings you choose to feature
- **Recent closings** — Sold homes you choose to feature
- **Payment methods** — Venmo, Zelle, Cash App, PayPal, or Stripe Checkout links

## Editing layout

From the section list you can:

- Drag sections to reorder them
- Toggle a section visible or hidden
- Click a section to customize it
- Remove a section
- Add another section that is not already on the page

Hidden sections stay in the builder but do not appear on the public page.

## Previewing

The preview panel shows how the page will render. Use the device controls to check:

- Desktop
- Tablet
- Mobile
- Card

The **Card** view is the compact scan-optimized contact card used by QR-code sharing.

## Publishing

Use **Publish** when the page is ready. Until the page is published, visitors who open the public URL see that the page is not set up yet. The owner can still preview the draft.

After publishing, the page can be opened from **View site**. Use **Unpublish** if you need to take the page offline.

## Analytics and SEO

The builder includes:

- Page views
- Unique visitors
- CTA clicks
- QR scans
- vCard downloads
- Link copies
- Lead submits
- View-to-lead conversion
- CTA and lead activity by preset
- Traffic source
- Daily activity chart

You can also set the page title, meta description, and social share image used when the Front Door URL is shared.
