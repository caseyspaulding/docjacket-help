# Your First Transaction

Step-by-step walkthrough of creating your first transaction in DocJacket

Let's walk through creating a transaction from scratch. By the end, you'll have a deal set up with contacts, key dates, and tasks — ready to manage.

## Two ways to create a transaction

From the **Transactions** page, click the **New Transaction** button in the top right. You've got two options:

- **Create Manually** — Fill in the details yourself
- **Upload & Extract** — Upload a contract PDF and let AI do the heavy lifting

new transaction button with dropdown options

### Option A: Upload & Extract (recommended)

This is the fastest way. Upload your contract PDF and DocJacket's AI will read through it and pull out:

- Buyer and seller names
- Property address
- Purchase price and earnest money
- Contract date, closing date, and key deadlines
- Contingency periods

1. Click **Upload & Extract**
2. Drag and drop your contract PDF (or click to browse)
3. Wait a moment while AI processes the document
4. Review the extracted data — make any corrections needed
5. Click **Create Transaction**

AI extraction review screen with extracted fields

### Option B: Create manually

If you don't have a contract handy or prefer to enter details yourself:

1. Click **Create Manually**
2. Fill in the basics — property address, transaction type, and side (buy/sell/both)
3. Add the purchase price and key dates
4. Click **Create**

You can always add more details later from the transaction overview.

## Add your contacts

Every deal has people involved. From the transaction overview, you'll see the **Contacts & Vendors** section on the right side. Add the key players:

- **Buyers and Sellers** — The principals in the deal
- **Agents** — The listing agent, buying agent, or both
- **Title Company** — Who's handling closing
- **Lender** — If there's financing involved
- **Other vendors** — Inspectors, surveyors, appraisers, etc.

You can pick from your existing contacts or create new ones on the fly.

contacts panel showing role slots

## Apply a checklist

Checklists are what set up the reusable workflow for your deal. From the **Timeline** tab:

1. Click **Apply Checklist**
2. Choose a checklist that matches your transaction type and state
3. Review the key dates, tasks, and options
4. Adjust any dates if needed

Your timeline now shows the important key dates from contract to closing, with automatic deadline calculations.

timeline tab with key dates

## Check your tasks

Head to the **Tasks** tab. If your checklist included tasks, they're already here — organized by due date with priorities color-coded:

- 🔴 **Urgent** — Do this now
- 🟠 **High** — Don't let this slip
- 🟡 **Medium** — Standard priority
- 🟢 **Low** — When you get to it

You can also add tasks manually with the quick-add bar at the top, or let AI generate a task checklist based on your transaction details.
