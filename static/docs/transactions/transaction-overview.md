# Transaction Overview

The Overview tab is where most TCs spend their time. It shows everything important about a deal on one page — no clicking through multiple tabs to find what you need.

## What's on the Overview

The Overview is customizable, so your organization may hide, pin, or collapse some sections. Common sections include:

### Key Date Timeline
The key-date timeline appears near the top of the overview when visible. Use **Generate Timeline** to calculate key dates and linked tasks from a checklist template, or add key dates manually. The timeline can show a pipeline view or a vertical checklist view.

### Risk Insights
When a transaction has a stored risk assessment, a risk chip appears in the transaction header. Click it to review the summary, risk factors, mitigations, recommendations, and to re-run the assessment.

### Waiting On
The headline blocker for the deal. Use it to show what the transaction is currently waiting on, such as client action, signatures, title, lender, inspection, or closing.

### Financial Summary
Purchase price, earnest money, loan amount, and other financial terms at a glance. Quick reference so you don't have to dig through the contract.

### Additional Details
Deal details that do not fit into the main summary cards, such as listing, occupancy, financing, and other transaction fields your organization tracks.

### Tasks
Your to-do list for this transaction. Add tasks, check them off, and track what's overdue. The progress bar shows how much is complete.

### Notes
Internal notes for your team. These are **private** — they're not visible to clients or agents on the portal.

### Property Images
Upload photos of the property. Star an image to feature it on reports and the client portal.

### Documents
Contracts, disclosures, and other files for this transaction. Track approval status and upload new documents right from here.

### Calendar Sync
If your account has Google Calendar connected, this card controls whether this transaction's key dates and overdue tasks appear on your synced calendar.

### Contingencies
Track configured contingency status throughout the deal. Mark each as Active, Satisfied, Waived, or Not Applicable as the transaction progresses.

### Repairs
Track common repair categories and their resolution status. Mark items as N/A, Pending, or Resolved, and add notes when needed.

### Messages
Recent emails related to this transaction. See unread counts and click through to the full message thread.

### Parties and Contacts
The people involved in the deal. The overview can show buyers, sellers, agents, title company, lender, vendors, and additional contacts, with links into the full Contacts tab.

### System Credentials
Store login details for MLS, tax portals, and other external systems used in this transaction. Keeps everything in one place so you're not hunting through emails for passwords.
