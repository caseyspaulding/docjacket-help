# Transaction Overview

The Overview tab is where most TCs spend their time. It shows everything important about a deal on one page — no clicking through multiple tabs to find what you need.

full transaction overview page

## What's on the Overview

Here's a breakdown of each section:

### Upcoming Key Dates
A visual snapshot of the next important dates at the top. Click any key date to jump into the full key date list for more details.

### AI Insights
If you've run an AI analysis, risk assessments and compliance checks appear here. Each issue has one-click actions you can approve or dismiss.

### Key Dates
Closing date, contract date, inspection deadline, and other important dates. These stay in sync when you update the transaction — change the closing date and downstream dates recalculate automatically.

### Financial Summary
Purchase price, earnest money, loan amount, and other financial terms at a glance. Quick reference so you don't have to dig through the contract.

### Tasks
Your to-do list for this transaction. Add tasks, check them off, and track what's overdue. The progress bar shows how much is complete.

### Notes
Internal notes for your team. These are **private** — they're not visible to clients or agents on the portal.

### Property Images
Upload photos of the property. Star an image to feature it on reports and the client portal.

### Documents
Contracts, disclosures, and other files for this transaction. Track approval status and upload new documents right from here.

### Key Date Timeline
Key deadlines for the transaction. Apply a checklist or add key dates manually. The "Next Up" badge highlights what's coming next.

### Contingencies
Track contingency status throughout the deal. Mark each as Active, Satisfied, or Waived as the transaction progresses.

### Repairs
Track repair requests and their resolution status. Add repair items from inspections and mark them as resolved.

### Messages
Recent emails related to this transaction. See unread counts and click through to the full message thread.

### Contacts & Vendors
All the people involved — buyers, sellers, agents, title company, lender, and third-party vendors like inspectors and surveyors. This column runs down the right side of the overview.

### System Credentials
Store login details for MLS, tax portals, and other external systems used in this transaction. Keeps everything in one place so you're not hunting through emails for passwords.
