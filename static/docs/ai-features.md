# AI Features

DocJacket uses in-app AI workflows to handle the tedious parts of transaction coordination — so you can focus on the work that actually needs a human touch.

Here's the important thing: **DocJacket separates AI suggestions from the final action.** Extraction results, review-queue drafts, and automation output are surfaced for review so you can approve, edit, reject, or save the work intentionally.

These are the AI features inside DocJacket. They are separate from **[AI Access](/docs/ai-access)**, which connects external assistants like Claude, ChatGPT, Codex, Cowork, and Gemini to your DocJacket data.

## What AI can do for you

- **Contract Extraction** — Upload a contract packet and AI pulls out names, dates, prices, terms, warnings, and source citations for review
- **Task Generation** — AI analyzes your transaction and suggests tasks you can select before adding
- **Risk Insights** — Review transaction-level risk assessments and document-level checks
- **Smart Document Splitting** — Upload a multi-document PDF and AI detects the documents, page ranges, and primary contract
- **Communication Drafting** — AI drafts emails and messages inside DocJacket composers or review queues before they send
