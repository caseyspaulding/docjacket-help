# Transactions

Transactions are the heart of DocJacket. Every real estate deal you manage — from the moment a contract is signed to the day it closes — lives here.

![Transactions page with filters, columns, saved views, and transaction rows](/img/transactions/transactions-list.png)

Your **Transactions** page is home base. It shows every deal you're managing in a searchable, filterable table. You can:

- **Search** by property address, buyer name, or seller name
- **Filter** by status, side, price range, closing date range, and other saved-view options
- **Switch between Transactions and Listings**
- **Open saved views** for repeat reporting layouts
- **Customize columns** to show exactly the info you care about
- **Export to CSV** for reporting or backup

Click any transaction to open it and see the full picture: overview, tasks, documents, contacts, messages, portal, forms, activity, reports, and calculators.
